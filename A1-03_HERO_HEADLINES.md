## Headline Principles

### Formula
```
[User role/audience] + [Verb/action] + [Outcome/benefit] + [Time element]
```

### What Works
✅ Lead with problem or outcome (not product name)
✅ Use power verbs (diagnose, identify, recover, prevent)
✅ Include time element (seconds, instantly, before)
✅ Specific and concrete (not "better" or "faster")
✅ Avoid jargon (no "orchestration", "pipeline", "evidence-first")
✅ Appeal to emotion (reduce stress, gain confidence, ship faster)

### What Doesn't Work
❌ Lead with product (Claude Debug Copilot, Paperclip)
❌ Use jargon (orchestration, evidence-first, verification)
❌ Vague benefits ("better UX", "more efficient")
❌ Too technical (4-agent pipeline, root cause analysis)
❌ No time element
❌ Doesn't connect to user pain point
